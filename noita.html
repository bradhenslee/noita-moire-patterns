Noita Eye Cloud Moiré Pattern Explorer

  1. On init, the 9 clouds are arranged vertically in default order in a single column in Layer 1.
    • Drag & drop clouds to change the vertical order.
    • Click "Save" to keep your custom order in local storage.
    • Left Click to rotate 90˚. (not savable yet)
    • Shift+Click to cause a cloud to overlap its neighbor above (1 click) or below (2 clicks), allowing exact "fitting" of clouds like W4 and E5. (not savable yet)
  2. You can save Layer 1 ordering to local storage. (Overlaps, rotations, and position saving TK)
  3. Click options to view mirrored and 90° rotated variants of Layer 1 (Layers 2 and 3/4).
  4. Add additional images (you may need to manually correct CSS scaling)
  5. Use the Up,Down,Left,and Right Arrow Keys to nudge layers 1px at a time.
